About

Christ College Of Engineering provides a wide range of opportunities to its students in the form of CCE Placements. Many popular companies are recruiting students from CCE every year.

Vision

To assist our students in preparing for a rewarding career and to initiate our students into their chosen career path by connecting them with prospective employers.

Mission

To motivate and guide the students into making smart career choices. To equip students with career-oriented skills. To connect the students to a rewarding career.

Objectives

To counsel the students about the available career options, identify their areas of interest and assist them in selecting a rewarding career. To collaborate with the faculty members in mapping the industry requirements with that of the interest areas of students. To provide professional training to students to increase their employability skills. To achieve maximum placement of students in reputed companies.
