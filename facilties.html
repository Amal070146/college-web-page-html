COMPUTER SOCIETY OF INDIA

Department of Computer Science & Engineering started in 2015 in Christ College of Engineering, which offers the undergraduate (B. Tech.) course. The annual intake of the B. Tech. program is 60 students and it is affiliated to Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University.
